ScanPDF

On-device document scanner - turns paper into multi-page PDF, no cloud required.

What it does

  • Scan any document: receipts, contracts, notes, whiteboards, ID cards.
  • Auto edge detection & perspective correction: powered by Google ML Kit Document Scanner running on-device.
  • Multi-page PDFs: combine several pages into a single document, then preview the first page.
  • Filters: Original, Black & White, Grayscale, Auto-enhance.
  • Gallery import: scan photos already on your device.
  • Instant share: send the PDF to any app via Android's system share sheet.
  • Privacy by design: no internet permission declared, no analytics, no ads, no tracking. All scans stay on your device.
